Payment Instructions

Payments are automatically settled every day at 08:00 UTC time. Transfers to your designated payout address are processed within four hours after settlement. Payments occur once per day and are irreversible.

For Pool Miners: Earnings are based on submitted shares and are paid daily according to the pool's settlement rules.

For Solo Miners: Payouts are made only when a block is successfully found and confirmed. No earnings are generated without a block.

Important: Before setting your payout address, please carefully verify that it is correct and valid. Once payments are sent, they cannot be modified, canceled, or recovered.

FAQ

How often are earnings calculated?

Earnings are calculated in real-time and settled daily based on confirmed block rewards.

What happens if I change my payout address?

Changes to your payout address are effective immediately. However, to ensure security, frequent changes may trigger additional verification.

What is the minimum payout amount?

There is no minimum payout limit. Once settlement is completed, payments are made automatically regardless of the amount.

What if my mining device goes offline?

If your miner goes offline, it will stop submitting shares. No earnings will be accumulated until the miner reconnects and resumes mining.

Can I mine with multiple devices using the same account?

Yes, you can connect multiple mining devices to the same account to increase your hashrate and potential rewards.

How long does it take for a block reward to be confirmed?

Block confirmations typically require a certain number of network confirmations. Rewards are credited once a block is fully confirmed by the blockchain network.
